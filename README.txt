ABOUT
----------------------------------------

Pack XP Optimizer helps you figure out the most efficient method of opening
packs based on your personal opening speed, inventory, and statistical
outcomes. By taking your individual stats into account, it calculates the
optimal approach to maximise your XP gains — taking the guesswork out of
pack opening strategy.

----------------------------------------
USAGE
----------------------------------------

Visit Pack XP Optimizer at https://leftykop.github.io/[repo-name]
No installation needed. It runs entirely in your browser.
