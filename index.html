How it works: each open is worth its tier multiplier (Regular 1x up to Rainbow 6x). Downgrading multiplies pack count more than it cuts per-card XP, so it always yields more total XP — but more opens, i.e. more time. The optimizer finds the deepest tier whose supply covers your whole session (your floor), then spends leftover packs upgrading some opens to higher tiers, capped at 3 rarities. Never upgrade packs in-game: it taxes ~66% of their value.
